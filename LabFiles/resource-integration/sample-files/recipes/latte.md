# Recipe: Café Latte

## Ingredients
- 18g espresso grounds
- 150ml whole milk (3.5% fat)
- Optional: flavoring syrup

## Instructions

1. **Prepare espresso** following espresso recipe
2. **Steam milk** to 60-65°C with microfoam
3. **Pour steamed milk** into cup with espresso
4. **Create latte art** with remaining foam
5. **Serve immediately** in 240ml cup

## Milk Steaming Process

1. **Purge steam wand** before use
2. **Insert wand** just below milk surface
3. **Create microfoam** for 3-5 seconds
4. **Heat milk** by moving wand deeper
5. **Stop at 65°C** to avoid scalding
6. **Tap pitcher** to settle foam
7. **Swirl milk** to integrate foam

## Latte Art Patterns

- **Heart**: Basic pattern for beginners
- **Rosetta**: Intermediate leaf pattern  
- **Swan**: Advanced decorative pattern
- **Tulip**: Stacked heart variation

## Variations

- **Iced Latte**: Use cold milk, serve over ice
- **Vanilla Latte**: Add 15ml vanilla syrup
- **Caramel Latte**: Add 15ml caramel syrup
- **Oat Milk Latte**: Substitute oat milk for dairy
