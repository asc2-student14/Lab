# Recipe: Cappuccino

## Ingredients
- 18g espresso grounds  
- 100ml whole milk
- Cocoa powder for dusting (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Prepare double espresso** (30ml)
2. **Steam milk** to create equal foam and steamed milk
3. **Pour steamed milk** first, then spoon foam on top
4. **Dust with cocoa** if desired
5. **Serve in 180ml cup**

## Traditional Proportions
- 1/3 espresso (30ml)
- 1/3 steamed milk (50ml) 
- 1/3 milk foam (50ml)
