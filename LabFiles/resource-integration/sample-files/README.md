# BeanBotics - Autonomous Robot Barista System

Welcome to BeanBotics, the future of automated coffee service!

## Overview

BeanBotics combines advanced robotics with precision brewing to deliver the perfect cup of coffee every time. Our AI-powered barista robots can:

- Recognize customer preferences through facial recognition
- Prepare 50+ different coffee beverages
- Maintain optimal brewing temperatures and timing
- Learn from customer feedback to improve recipes

## Quick Start

1. Power on your BeanBotics unit
2. Run the calibration sequence: `python calibrate.py`
3. Load coffee beans and milk reservoirs
4. Start the service: `python main.py --mode=service`
